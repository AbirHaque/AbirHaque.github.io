SwimDB:
I have been working on this app with my school's swim coach since February 2020. An Android prototype was completed in May 2020 with its iOS counterpart bound to be completed by July 2020. Features of the completed prototype include: Coaches/trainers storing swimmers' times, creating group workout sets, and calculating workout intervals of individual swimmers.

Bangla Alphabet App: Product
Development of this app lasted from September to October 2019. I created the app with two intentions: to have an app that provides interactive Bangla alphabet charts to aide in Bangla literacy and to have a submission for the 2019 KS-04 Congressional App Challenge. While I consider this app to be rudimentary, it was the biggest project I ever completed in 2019 and won the challenge.
